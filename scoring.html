LexTurk Lexical test for learners of Turkish

LexTurk uses the same scoring methodology as LexTALE1 (Lemhöfer & Broersma, 2012). The averaged % of correct responses for each participant is calculated following the formula below:

LexTurk score = ((Ncorrect W/40*100) + (Ncorrect NW/20*100)) / 2

Scores range from 0 to 100%. The highest score can only be achieved if the participant correctly identifies all words and rejects all non-words.
